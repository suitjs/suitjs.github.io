About

Plain Simple

Single script framework with a very easy workflow, basically just name the elements and listen the events at a switch case.

Comfortable

Thin layer on top of vanilla js, offers the bare minimum to speed up development and don't demand extravagant concepts or force the use of all functionalities.

Complete

It covers the most important front-end tasks like, event handling, controllers communication, HTTP requests and DOM manipulation.
